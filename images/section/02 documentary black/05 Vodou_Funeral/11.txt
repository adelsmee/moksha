Ceremonious dancers performing a Vodou dance for the Lwa.

Abeille’s brother places an oil lamp on his head.  He’d been visited by Abeille’s spirit who asked him to place the oil lamp upon the forehead of the body during the ceremony, once the coffin was closed Abeille’s brother took the oil lamp and held it above his own head, after which he carried it for the rest of the day.

A mourner collapses in possession as the Vodou Empress helps to bring her to.
